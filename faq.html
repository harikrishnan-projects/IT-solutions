General FAQs

Answers to the most common questions about our IT services. Click a question to expand the answer.

What IT services do you offer?

We offer managed IT services, cloud migration, cybersecurity, DevOps, custom software development, and 24/7 support plans tailored to your needs.

How does your support process work?

Our support process begins with a ticket system and triage, followed by remote troubleshooting and on-site visits when required. We provide SLA-based response and resolution times.

What industries do you serve?

We serve finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, education, startups and SMEs. We adapt security and compliance controls per industry requirements.

Do you provide 24/7 customer support?

Yes — 24/7 support is available as a paid plan option. We also provide priority SLAs for critical incidents.

How can I contact your technical team?

You can contact us via the support portal, email at support@example.com, or call our hotline available on the Contact page.

Technical & Service FAQs

Deep technical answers about our products, security, and development practices.

How secure is your cloud service?

We use multi-layer security: network segmentation, IAM, encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+), regular penetration testing, and SOC/ISO-aligned controls. We also offer dedicated VPCs and zero-trust configurations for enterprise clients.

Do you provide data backup and recovery?

Yes. We provide scheduled incremental backups, point-in-time recovery (PITR) for databases, and tested disaster recovery (DR) runbooks. Retention policies are configurable per SLA.

What technologies do you use in development?

Frontend: React, Next.js. Backend: Python (FastAPI), Node.js. Databases: PostgreSQL, Redis. CI/CD: GitHub Actions, Docker, Kubernetes for orchestration. Cloud: AWS/Azure/GCP depending on client needs.

How does your IT consulting process work?

We start with discovery and assessment, then architecture & roadmap, implementation sprints, testing, and ongoing optimization. Deliverables and milestones are agreed upfront in the SOW.

Do you offer custom software development?

Yes. We offer custom development from proof-of-concept to production, with iterative sprints, code reviews, and automated testing. We deliver docs, CI/CD pipelines, and handover training.
